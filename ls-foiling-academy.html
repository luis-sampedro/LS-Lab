LS Foiling Academy (LSFA)

This area is still in development. The goal of LS Foiling Academy is to offer a complete space combining structured online learning with hands-on practical sessions on the water. Step-by-step content, real demonstrations, and sessions aboard high-performance foiling boats will shape the core of the experience.

First course — How to Sail a Moth

The first module focuses on high-performance foiling using an International Moth. I provide the boat and deliver on-shore briefings before practice.

The course will include:

  • Understanding the basics of foil behaviour and trim
  • Pre-session briefings and dockside demonstrations
  • Progressive on-water sessions adapted to each student
  • Feedback, improvement tracking and personalised tips

Designed for sailors who want to take their performance to the next level, from first flights to stable hydrofoiling.

More courses and experiences will be added soon.
