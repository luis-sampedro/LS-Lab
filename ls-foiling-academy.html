LS Foiling Academy (LSFA)

This area is still in development. My goal is to build a complete online & hands-on training suite inspired by platforms like NavalApp.com (online courses) and Zarpar.eu (practical sailing experiences).

First course – How to Sail a Moth

The first module in the academy will focus on high–performance foiling techniques using a International Moth. I will provide the boat and run introductory talks before on-water sessions.

The idea is to bring people from zero to flying confidently, combining:

  • Understanding of foil dynamics
  • Dock-side demonstrations and safety briefing
  • On-water hands-on training
  • Progress tracking

More courses and experiences coming soon.
