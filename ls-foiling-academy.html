LS Foiling Academy (LSFA)

This area is still in development. LS Foiling Academy will combine structured online lessons with hands-on practical sessions on the water. Real demonstrations, training tools and high-performance foiling boats will form the core of the experience.

#1 — How to Sail a Moth

The first module focuses on high-performance foiling using an International Moth. I provide the boat and deliver on-shore briefings before on-water sessions.

The course will include:

  • Understanding foil behaviour, balance and trim
  • Dockside demonstrations and safety briefing
  • Progressive on-water sessions tailored to each sailor
  • Technique feedback, improvement tracking and personalised tips

Designed for sailors who want to progress from first flights to stable, confident hydrofoiling.

More courses and experiences will be added soon.
